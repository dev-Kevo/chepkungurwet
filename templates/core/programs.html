Economic Empowerment Program

Our economic empowerment initiatives provide women and youth with the tools and resources needed to break the cycle of poverty. Through comprehensive training and financial support, we help community members establish sustainable businesses and gain financial independence.

HEALTH & DIGNITY

Menstrual Health Program

Breaking the silence and stigma around menstruation, our program provides education, resources, and support to ensure girls and women can manage their periods with dignity and without interruption to their education or daily lives.

Our Approach

  • Community education
  • Pad distribution drives
  • Safe spaces for discussion

Program Impact

  • 5,000+ girls supported
  • 30+ schools engaged
  • 95% school attendance improvement
