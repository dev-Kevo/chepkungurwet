WHO WE ARE

Chepkungurwet Foundation

A grassroots organization committed to empowering women, children/youth, and families across Kenya. We focus on holistic, community-driven solutions that uplift lives and restore dignity.

Economic Empowerment

Supporting income-generating activities and financial literacy for women and youth.

School Mentorship & Education

Promoting access to education, life skills, and mentorship for vulnerable students.

Menstrual Hygiene Awareness

Breaking stigma through menstrual health education and pad distribution.

Decent Housing & Sanitation

Enhancing living conditions through improved shelter and hygiene infrastructure.

OUR MISSION

Creating Lasting Change

Chepkungurwet Foundation is dedicated to the holistic transformation of rural families and communities through sustainable development initiatives that address economic, social, and environmental challenges.

OUR JOURNEY

Milestones & Achievements

2010: Foundation Established

Founded by a group of local leaders to address poverty in rural Kenya.

2013: First School Built

Constructed our first primary school serving 150 children in Chepkungurwet village.

2016: Economic Empowerment Launch

Started microfinance program helping 50 women start businesses.

2020: COVID-19 Response

Distributed emergency food and hygiene supplies to 500 families during pandemic.
